## The Three Entities

### 1. ENTELECH - The Consulting Agency

**Purpose**: Client acquisition and engagement management

**Core Functions**:
- Find RevOps and consulting clients needing AI transformation
- Sell scopes of work (automation, AI agents, enterprise SaaS features)
- Manage client relationships and expectations
- Oversee project delivery and quality
- Handle partnership agreements (revshare/equity splits)

**Revenue Model**:
- Consulting fees from client engagements
- Retains 30-40% of project value for client acquisition and management
- Partnership equity/revshare from long-term client relationships

**Target Clients**:
- Enterprise SaaS companies needing agentic features
- SMBs requiring workflow automation
- RevOps departments seeking business process optimization
- Companies wanting in-house AI capabilities (DIY training)

**Example Engagement**:
> Enterprise SaaS company needs AI-powered customer support agent integrated with their existing platform. ENTELECH scopes the project at $50K, manages the client relationship, and coordinates delivery through AIIA consultant.

---

### 2. AIIA - The Talent Platform

**Purpose**: Recruit, train, vet, and place AI consultants

**Core Functions**:
- Source aspiring AI consultants and implementation specialists
- Train candidates on practical AI implementation (Claude API, n8n, custom agents)
- Vet consultants through portfolio projects and KPI benchmarks
- Match consultants to ENTELECH client engagements
- Monitor performance and ensure quality delivery

**Revenue Model**:
- Placement fees (20% of consultant's project value)
- Training course revenue from aspiring consultants
- Subscription tiers for job board and certification access
- Partnership splits on successful engagements

**Talent Pipeline**:
1. **Recruitment**: Source candidates interested in AI consulting
2. **Training**: 8-12 week curriculum on AI implementation
3. **Vetting**: Portfolio project + KPI benchmarks + certification
4. **Placement**: Match to ENTELECH client needs
5. **Monitoring**: Track performance and continuous improvement

**Example Flow**:
> Aspiring AI consultant completes AIIA training, builds portfolio project implementing Claude API automation, gets certified, then gets placed on ENTELECH's $50K customer support agent project. Consultant earns $25K, AIIA earns $10K placement fee.

---

### 3. ECHELON - The Software Platform

**Purpose**: Custom software and AI agent builds (when needed)

**Core Functions**:
- Build custom AI agents for complex implementations
- Develop enterprise SaaS features and integrations
- Provide technical infrastructure for challenging projects
- Support AIIA consultants with advanced technical needs
- White-label software platform for client deployments

**Revenue Model**:
- Project-based fees for custom software builds
- SaaS subscription fees for platform features
- Technical consulting for complex architectures
- White-label licensing

**Use Cases**:
- Complex multi-agent systems beyond consultant capabilities
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure requirements
- Custom integrations with legacy enterprise systems
- Advanced AI features requiring R&D

**Example Scenario**:
> ENTELECH client needs a complex multi-agent system with custom UX, real-time data processing, and enterprise security. AIIA consultant handles scoping and client communication, but ECHELON team builds the technical infrastructure and advanced features.

## Three Service Models

### DFY (Do For You) - Full Implementation

**When to Use**:
- Client lacks in-house technical team
- Needs working solution quickly
- Wants ongoing support and maintenance

**Delivery Model**:
1. ENTELECH scopes project and manages client
2. AIIA provides implementation consultant
3. Consultant builds complete solution
4. ECHELON supports if advanced features needed
5. Client receives turnkey system

**Revenue Split Example** ($50K project):
- ENTELECH: $15K (30%) - Client acquisition and management
- Consultant: $25K (50%) - Full implementation
- AIIA: $10K (20%) - Placement and monitoring
- ECHELON: $0 (not needed for this project)

---

### DIY (Do It Yourself) - Training & Enablement

**When to Use**:
- Client has in-house team
- Wants to build internal AI capabilities
- Prefers knowledge transfer over outsourcing

**Delivery Model**:
1. ENTELECH scopes training engagement
2. AIIA provides consultant with teaching experience
3. Consultant trains client's team on AI implementation
4. Client team builds and maintains their own systems
5. Consultant provides ongoing advisory support

**Revenue Split Example** ($30K training engagement):
- ENTELECH: $10K (33%) - Client acquisition and management
- Consultant: $15K (50%) - Training delivery
- AIIA: $5K (17%) - Placement and monitoring

---

### Hybrid - Consulting + Implementation

**When to Use**:
- Client has some technical capability
- Needs expert guidance + critical component builds
- Wants to maintain and extend systems in-house

**Delivery Model**:
1. ENTELECH scopes hybrid engagement
2. AIIA provides consultant with consulting + technical skills
3. Consultant designs architecture and builds core features
4. Client team implements remaining features
5. Consultant provides ongoing advisory and code review

**Revenue Split Example** ($40K hybrid project):
- ENTELECH: $12K (30%) - Client acquisition and management
- Consultant: $20K (50%) - Consulting + core implementation
- AIIA: $8K (20%) - Placement and monitoring

## Partnership Agreements

### Standard Partnership Model

**For Each Client Engagement**:
- ENTELECH and selected partner (from AIIA network) co-deliver
- Revenue split per agreement (typically 30/50/20)
- ENTELECH handles client relationship
- Partner handles implementation/training
- AIIA provides quality assurance and monitoring

### Long-Term Partnership (Revshare/Equity)

**For Strategic Clients**:
- ENTELECH identifies high-value client with recurring needs
- Selects partner from AIIA network based on expertise
- Negotiate revshare or equity split on ongoing relationship
- Partner becomes dedicated to that client
- AIIA continues monitoring and support

**Example**:
> Enterprise SaaS client needs ongoing AI feature development. ENTELECH brings client, AIIA consultant implements features monthly. Instead of per-project fees, they agree to 40% ENTELECH / 40% Consultant / 20% AIIA split of all revenue from that client over 2 years.

## Value Proposition by Stakeholder

### For Clients

**Single Point of Contact** (ENTELECH):
- One agency relationship for all AI needs
- Consistent quality through vetted AIIA network
- Flexibility: DFY, DIY, or Hybrid based on needs
- Ongoing support and relationship management

**Access to Vetted Talent** (AIIA):
- Pre-screened consultants with proven skills
- KPI-tracked performance
- Replacement guarantee if consultant doesn't perform
- Portfolio of past work and references

**Technical Excellence** (ECHELON when needed):
- Advanced features beyond consultant capabilities
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure and security
- Custom integrations and complex architectures
- R&D for cutting-edge AI implementations

---

### For AIIA Consultants

**Steady Pipeline** (ENTELECH):
- Regular client engagements without sales effort
- Professional scoping and client management
- Protected from scope creep and difficult clients
- Focus on delivery, not business development

**Training & Credibility** (AIIA):
- Practical skills training on in-demand technologies
- Certification that carries weight with clients
- Mentorship from experienced consultants
- Community of peers for knowledge sharing

**Career Growth**:
- Progression from small DFY projects to large hybrid engagements
- Path to becoming ENTELECH partner with equity/revshare
- Specialization tracks (verticals, tech stacks)
- Potential to join ECHELON team for advanced R&D

---

### For ENTELECH (The Agency)

**Scalable Delivery** (AIIA):
- Access to vetted talent without hiring overhead
- Flexible capacity based on client pipeline
- Quality assurance through AIIA monitoring
- Consultant replacement if performance issues

**Technical Capability** (ECHELON):
- Bid on complex projects beyond consultant skills
- Differentiation from other consulting agencies
- R&D for proprietary IP and competitive advantage
- White-label platform for rapid client deployments

**Revenue Growth**:
- 30-40% margin on all client engagements
- Equity/revshare on long-term partnerships
- Scalable without proportional cost increases
- Multiple revenue streams (consulting, training, SaaS)
